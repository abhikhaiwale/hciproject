CAR PARKING

This documentation is intended as an introductory literature for anyone who is interested in our product. This document is in no way meant to serve as a comprehensive guide and should be mistaken as such. Our product, as the name suggests, is a software application that will allow people to “reserve” parking spots in a parking spot similar to how people reserve seats in a cinema hall. We, as owners of vehicles, came to the realization that finding a parking spot for one’s personal vehicle is a very arduous and time consuming task. If one cannot find such a spot then they have no choice but to watch helplessly as their car gets towed. We wish to prevent such an event from happening and as such, we have implemented a grid based system on a parking spot to recognize and categorize individual spots. Users can then proceed to select a spot and reserve it for a specific time before their arrival.
